Our Story

Sunrise General Store started as a small family business with a simple dream - to serve our local community with quality products at fair prices. What began in 2015 as a modest neighborhood store has grown into a trusted name, known for personal service and reliability.

When the world went digital, we decided to bring our store online to better serve our customers. Now you can shop from the comfort of your home and still receive the same personal attention and care that has made us a beloved part of the community.
